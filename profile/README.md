# DomainTools Investigations IOC Library
## ⚙️About DomainTools Investigations
DomainTools Investigations (DTI) is a research program focused on preventing, mitigating, and investigating domain and DNS-based threats. The DTI Indicator of Compromise (IOC) Library is a collection of all IOCs our researchers and analysts have identified in our work. This library includes domains, crypto wallets, trackers, malware, webshells, personas, and any other indicators we find associated with specific campaigns and threat actors. 
## 📈How Our Data is Published
Our repositories contain all IOCs found in our published research. These are provided in the library as .csv and .json files. Links to the associated research can also be found with each data set.
